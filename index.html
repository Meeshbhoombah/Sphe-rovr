Make the entire world your playground.

The Sphero uses a "Chariot," that holds an Android phone that is connected to an Oculus. Using directional sensors on the Oculus to control the Sphero's acceleration and direction, Sphe-rovr enables users to move in any direction (expect up or down). The Sphe-rovr also live streams video from the Android phone to the Oculus for a fully immersive experience. With the Sphero, Oculus, and Android phone you can literally, "be the ball."

Works with Firefox OS

Pushing VR to its boundaries

We took a Sphero and a Oculus, two of the world's most fascinating technologies, and put them together. In doing so we effectively created a first person point of a view just six inches above the ground. The use cases are endless, its up to you to discover them.
